DMA Support

Following the successful rollout of the global EMS platform delivered by findev, the business expanded its offering to include Direct Market Access (DMA) for clients.

Key Challenges

  • High load: ~10 million trades per day
  • Strict latency requirements: Sub-1ms pass-through latency
  • Flexible client connectivity
  • Client risk & limit controls

Implementation Approach

  • Still maintained a single codebase for the platform
  • Deployed horizontally scalable, separate DMA instances
  • Redesigned core components to meet high-load and low-latency demands

Thanks to the platform's initial robust architecture, these enhancements were delivered within an acceptable time-to-market window. The DMA functionality successfully launched and was well-received by clients.
