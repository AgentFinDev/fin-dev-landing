how findev helps today's technology leaders

Knowledge

Our highly specialized fintech expertise equips findev to engineer and deliver next-generation software today — software ready to handle whatever tomorrow may bring. We have deep experience building pragmatic technology solutions that work efficiently for multiple players on The Street, giving us the knowledge, confidence and ability to do the same for your organization.

Vision

Findev teams have seen and done a great deal in fintech software over decades — experiences that shape our vision of the future and inform every solution we deliver. We've witnessed major shifts in technology firsthand, driven by increasing volumes, regulatory pressure, cloud migration, AI, and more. That long view gives findev the ability to anticipate future challenges the financial technology sector may present in an ever-changing market. Findev helps you see beyond the hype and use evolving technologies for greater business impact.

Results

Findev has a proven track record of successful implementations, and our reputation is easily verified. Here is a sampling of the results we are known for accomplishing day-to-day:

  • Performance enhancements to trading systems
  • Elimination of legacy-based system constraints
  • Improved ability to handle increasing trade and data volumes
  • Reduced vendor dependency and related risks (such as single point of failure, inflationary pricing, custom stack, and more)
  • Workflow automation in a systematic way, with or without AI
  • Infrastructure modernization in-flight, whether by rebuilding on-prem or performed via full or hybrid migration to the cloud
  • Development of execution platforms and analytic tools from scratch
